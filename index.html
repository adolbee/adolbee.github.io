My Tunisia Internship

October 27th, 2022

My love for travel and technology coincided once again. I 'd had experience in helping people with just the use of my laptop and love of learning before, but now it had led me to an IT internship in Sousse, Tunisia. I spent one month learning from an amazing team of techies how to code (with no previous experience) a one page website! I loved it. I was so intimidated at first but they showed me what an amazing community the tech world is.

I'm so grateful for that first experience and it's something I'll never forget.
